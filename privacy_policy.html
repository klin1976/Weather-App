Privacy Policy / 隱私權政策

English

This Privacy Policy describes how the Weather App ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information Collection and Use

Our application requests Location Permission solely for the purpose of fetching local weather data through the Open-Meteo API. We do not collect, store, share, or sell any personal data or location information.

2. Third-Party Services

The app uses the Open-Meteo API to retrieve weather and air quality information. Please refer to Open-Meteo's privacy policy for their data handling practices.

3. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.

繁體中文

本隱私權政策說明天氣應用程式（「我們」）如何收集、使用和保護您的資訊。

1. 資訊收集與使用

我們的應用程式要求位置權限，僅用於透過 Open-Meteo API 獲取當地天氣資料。我們不會收集、儲存、分享或出售任何個人資料或位置資訊。

2. 第三方服務

本應用程式使用 Open-Meteo API 獲取天氣及空氣品質資訊。有關他們的資料處理方式，請參閱 Open-Meteo 的隱私權政策。

3. 聯絡我們

如果您對本隱私權政策有任何疑問，請與我們聯絡。
